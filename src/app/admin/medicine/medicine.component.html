# Medicine About Who can? Who cannot? How & when to take? Side Effects Action
1 Paracetamol Paracetamol is a common painkiller used to treat aches and pain. It can also be used to reduce a high temperature. Most people aged 16 and over can take paracetamol. You can also take it if you are pregnant or breastfeeding. Paracetamol is not suitable for some people. To make sure it's safe for you, tell your doctor or pharmacist if you.. The usual dose for adults is either 500mg or 1g. Paracetamol very rarely causes side effects if you take it at the right dosage.
